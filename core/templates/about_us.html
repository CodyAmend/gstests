About us

Hope and a Helping Hand.

Good Shepherd Food Pantry is a non-profit organization that provides food, toiletries, and other items to those in need. We opened our doors in 2011 to serve those in the Omaha metro area who struggle with food insecurity. Since opening our doors, we've served over 750 individuals and families. We strive to always treat our clients with dignity and respect. Our mission is to serve the needs of the community long into the future.
